About SchoolManage

SchoolManage is a school management system by SlimCyberTech — built to help Ugandan secondary schools run on reliable digital records instead of paper.

SlimCyberTech

SlimCyberTech builds software for organisations that need practical, dependable tools. Our tagline — “Building the future with code.” — reflects a focus on shipping systems that work in the real world, not slide decks.

With SchoolManage, we apply that approach to Ugandan school administration: O-Level CBC assessment, A-Level UNEB grading, student records, fees in Ugandan shillings, and role-based dashboards for the people who run a school every day.

Our mission

Digitise school administration for Ugandan secondary schools — so headteachers, teachers, and bursars spend less time on manual paperwork and more time supporting learners.

Why we built this

Many secondary schools still rely on paper registers, handwritten report cards, and spreadsheets that do not talk to each other. When assessment policy shifts — CBC competency ratings and project work at O-Level, UNEB points and divisions at A-Level — manual processes become harder to sustain and easier to get wrong.

SchoolManage brings enrolment, assessment entry, fee collection, and report generation into one system. Teachers enter scores once; the platform applies the right grading rules; headteachers approve reports before they go out; bursars track who has paid — all with clear roles so staff see only what they need.

We designed the product around workflows described by school administrators and the official assessment frameworks schools already follow — not around generic templates that ignore CBC and UNEB requirements.

We are happy to walk through the system and answer questions about CBC, UNEB, or fees setup for your school.
