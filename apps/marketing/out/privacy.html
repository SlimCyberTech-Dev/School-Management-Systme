Privacy Policy

How SlimCyberTech handles personal data when you use SchoolManage.

1. Introduction

SlimCyberTech (“we”, “us”) operates SchoolManage, a school management platform used by Ugandan secondary schools. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information processed through the service.

2. Data we process

Depending on how your school uses the platform, we may process:

  • Staff account details (name, email, role, login activity)
  • Student records (name, class, assessment results, attendance, photos where uploaded)
  • Fee and payment information recorded by the school
  • Technical logs (IP address, browser type, API request metadata for security)

3. Legal framework

We aim to comply with the Uganda Data Protection and Privacy Act, 2019 and applicable regulations. Schools using SchoolManage act as data controllers for their learners and staff; SlimCyberTech typically acts as a data processor when hosting the service on the school's behalf. Specific controller/processor arrangements should be confirmed in your service agreement.

4. How we use data

Personal data is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the school management service
  • Authenticate users and enforce role-based access
  • Generate academic and financial reports requested by authorised staff
  • Protect the security and integrity of the platform

5. Data retention and security

Data is retained for as long as the school's account is active and as required by applicable law or contractual obligations. We apply technical measures including tenant isolation, access controls, and encrypted transport in production deployments. Details of retention periods and subprocessors should be added here after legal review.

6. Your rights

Under the Uganda Data Protection and Privacy Act, 2019, data subjects may have rights to access, rectify, or erase personal data, subject to lawful exceptions. Schools should direct learner and parent requests through their own data protection contact; SlimCyberTech will assist schools as processor where applicable.

7. Contact

For privacy enquiries, contact SlimCyberTech via the contact page pending appointment of a formal data protection contact.
